Artist Notes

About Her Work

Her paintings grow out of ordinary, meaningful things: tending plants, sitting by a window in changing weather, and collecting memories from people she loves.

She blends natural textures with human gestures to make artwork that feels personal, grounded, and quietly hopeful.

At a glance

  • Mediums: Acrylic, ink, natural pigments, mixed media
  • Favorite themes: Home, growth, memory, belonging
  • Based in: New York, NY
  • Available for: Commissions & cozy exhibitions
