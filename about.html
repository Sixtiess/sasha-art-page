About My Work

This website is my personal art journal. I use it to document pieces, save thoughts, and keep a record of how my style changes over time.

I live in Santa Barbara, so the work naturally leans into local textures: coastal fog, chaparral hillsides, palm silhouettes, and golden-hour light.

Quick notes

  • Mediums: Mixed media, acrylic, ink
  • Theme: Nature, memory, everyday life
  • Status: Ongoing personal project
  • Purpose: Share process and stories
